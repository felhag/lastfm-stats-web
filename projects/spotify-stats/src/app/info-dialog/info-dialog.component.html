Retrieve data from Spotify

Spotify keeps track of all your plays. Unfortunately it doesn't provide a decent way to retrieve this data programmatically so you'll have to request this data manually. You can do this on the spotify privacy page.

Note: When you use the "Download your data" tool it will only provide streaming history for the past year. If you send an email to privacy@spotify.com it includes all your streaming history!

After you received an email (takes about a week) you can download the zip and upload my_spotify_data.zip here.
